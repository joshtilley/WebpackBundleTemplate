BSP

British Social-Populists

As we head towards a new era of politics, defined by the birth of Post-Truth politics and algorithmic entrenchment, it is vital that alternative voices are heard.

The Right is gaining ground by making positive appeals to broad reactionary sentiments whilst the left's most prominent advocates engage in critique or mere punditry.

The UK needs a populist left that rallies around a coherent and minimalist set of positive ambitions.
